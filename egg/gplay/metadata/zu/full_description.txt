Shoot Dinosaur Amaqanda kuyinto umdlalo uthemba kokhela kumnandi ukudlala imidlalo zakudala njengoba ingane. Ukukhuluma izingane, lokhu umdlalo Kulula bangene netimphawu ihluzo ezimibalabala owawuzovumelana yimuphi Gamer abasha. Kukhona 500+ iphazili amazinga e Shoot Dinosaur Amaqanda futhi ngenkathi umdlalo luqala mnandi futhi kulula, maduze uthola nzima. Lena Classic Arcade game lokho owawuklanyelwe ukuba ukhange abadlali ezikhululekile nalabo ngifuna ezinye ISIFISO kanye. Ingabe sokugcwalisa lezo sihlose? Funda ku ukubona ukuthi sicabangani.
Njenganoma imidlalo kakhulu ku-Android, kuwumqondo omuhle ukuqala ukulanda ke kusukela ku-Google Play. Khona-ke, uzokhokhiswa iphonswe umdlalo omusha.
Ukulawula umdlalo kulula pretty, konke okudingayo ukuze ukwenze nje ukuba uthinte ohlangothini ofuna iqanda ukuxoshwa. Ungabona okuyinto umbala imayelana ukuba umkhankaso ngaphambi badonsa. Inhloso ukufanisa imibala kubakhiphe off the ipulangwe phezulu.
Ungabona ukuthi iyiphi ezingeni okuyo ekhoneni elingezansi kwesokunxele kwesikrini. Izinto luqale lula ngempela, kodwa amazinga musa ukuqala ukuza ngamaphetheni nzima ukwazi ukuthi uzodlala indlela engcono kakhulu ukususa bonke.
Umdlalo akukuniki inkomba kakhulu kangakanani eside awusenalo, noma zingaki ezinye amaqanda une, kodwa abanye abadlali ukuthi kuyoba ushintsho wamukelekile ijubane futhi ubavumele nje wenze umdlalo othandwa yibona.
Abanye amazinga ungakwazi kalula asuse iningi amaqanda kusukela ipulangwe phezulu endaweni ukudlala, kodwa ezinye izikhathi kungase kudingeke ucabange kancane kusheshe.
Uma ukusula ezingeni. uzoba aphathwe ku-dinosaur cute little in ukugubha.
Okuphambene uya lapho amaqanda udlule emideni emaqatjhazi futhi wehluleka ukususa ezingeni. Zombili lezi anezele nice abancane, futhi uzokwenza nokufeyila ezingeni akukubi kangako izingane.
Shoot Dinosaur Amaqanda kuyobenza babe izingane kanye abadlali ezikhululekile. Azisekho powerups ezikhethekile noma okunje, futhi umdlalo zakudala abaningi kithi owayazi nabathandwayo edlule ngokuyisisekelo. Lokho into embi Yiqiniso, njengoba labo abaye ngifuna into enhle futhi abayitoho uzothola mnandi ngempela. Kuba njalo enhle ukuthola umdlalo ngiyakwazi batusa ukuba abazali ukuthi akuyona uhlobo oluthile isihloko kwezemfundo futhi akuyona isidina. Lena fun for bonke ubudala, ningalenzi iphutha, kodwa ihluzo agqamile kumnandi nemisindo wenze lokhu umdlalo kahle uyakufanelekela ukuba izingane.
